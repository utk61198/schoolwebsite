Welcome

Established for more than 20 years and located in Ghaziabad, our school aims for the nourishment of mind and holistic development of our students uder rich tutelage. The school aims at imparting quality education and creating all rounded individuals for the society. Our goal is to inculcate traditional values in our students along with knowledge and skills of modern practices. A blend of extra curriculars along with academics help our students to excel in the competetive environment of modern society.
